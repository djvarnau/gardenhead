Our Beers

Jazzsaw

IPA, 7.5%

Our flagship beer is a west-coast style IPA inspired by the delicious beers of Portland, OR. Loaded with citra and centennial hops and perfectly-balanced, it's the perfect beer for all seasons.


Saison.com

Belgian Saison, 6.5%

We like our beers hoppy, and this saison is no exception. It's got a strong Belgian nose and a subtle hoppy citrus flavor. It's just the kind of beer you'd like to drink sitting on your front-porch in the middle of summer.


Dad Abs

Belgian Quadrupel, 10%

If Saison.com is the perfect summer beer, Dad Abs is your companion through the coldest parts of the midwestern winter. The huge ABV is supported by a classic Belgian malt backbone.


Thick Boy

Milk Stout, 5%

You've had a milk stout before, right? This pretty much tastes like that.


Crandis

Scotch Ale w/vanilla and cinnamon, 8%

Crandis is our Christmas gift to you. The perfect amount of vanilla with just a touch of cinnamon makes this beer perfect for a night at home, cuddling in front of a fire with whoever you like cuddling with.
